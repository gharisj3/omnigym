FAQ

Questions gym owners ask before switching

The core question is usually not whether the software has billing or check-ins. It is whether the AI actually reduces workload and improves retention in a way staff will use.

How quickly can a gym start using OmniGym?
Most gyms can get live quickly because the platform is browser-based and the setup centers on member import, team roles, plans, and branch structure rather than a custom technical deployment.
Is the AI actually useful for coaches, or is it just a demo feature?
The AI is designed around repeatable operational pain: program creation, churn detection, meal logging, and plan refinement. Each of those removes a real time cost or decision bottleneck from your team.
Can owners still review what the AI is doing?
Yes. OmniGym is built so the team can review AI output, accept or adjust recommendations, and keep human control over member-facing decisions.

No. The goal is the opposite. Check-ins become faster, nutrition logging becomes easier, and member communication stays cleaner because fewer actions depend on manual admin work.
